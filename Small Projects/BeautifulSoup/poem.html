"In the Absence of You"

In the quiet depths of longing's hue,
A poem blooms, as I'm missing you.
Through empty spaces, my heart does roam,
Seeking solace in memories of home.

Your laughter echoes within my soul,
A melody that I yearn to behold.
The touch of your hand, the warmth of your embrace,
I yearn for the moments we used to chase.

Days pass by like fleeting whispers in the wind,
While I'm left here, longing to rescind
The distance that separates our beating hearts,
Yearning for the closeness that love imparts.

The world seems dimmer, colors less bright,
In the absence of you, my guiding light.
Every step I take feels incomplete,
For your presence, my soul continues to entreat.

Yet, in the midst of this ache and despair,
I find solace knowing you still care.
For even when apart, our love remains,
Bound by an unbreakable, invisible chain.

Until the day when we're reunited once more,
I'll hold your memory close, to the core.
And through the empty spaces that divide,
I'll keep missing you, with love as my guide.
